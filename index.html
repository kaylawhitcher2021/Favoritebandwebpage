Who is Eric Church?

Artist Info

Born May 3, 1977 in Granite Falls North Carolina, Eric CHurch is a country/country rock singer and songwriter. At age thirteen Church bought his first guiter and started producing music on his own. By senior year he was playing covers of Jimmy Buffet and songs of his own at a local bar. After graduating highschool at South Caldwell High schoola and getting a degree in makreting from Appalachian State Univerisity, Church made his way to Nashville with the finanical baking from his fahter. Nashville allowed Church to create connects, but more importantly it allowed him to focus adn develop his songwriting ability.

Like most artist Eric Church world was rocked by Covid-19. So what has he been doing durignt his time? Church contiunes to work on his new albums that are going to hopefully be realised soon.
